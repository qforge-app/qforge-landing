How It Works

Four-Layer Proof-of-Work Grading

Not a quiz. A multi-layer cryptographic proof that you understand quantum computing.

1
Write Real Circuits
Submit Qiskit, Cirq, or PennyLane code. Each assignment has personalised parameters — your Grover oracle marks a different state than anyone else's.
2
Simulator Execution
Your circuit runs on a real quantum simulator. Scored by TVD (Total Variation Distance) against the exact expected output — not keywords.

Knowledge Challenge
Two questions generated about YOUR specific code choices. 2-minute timer. Proves you understand what you built, not just copied it.

Your cert's Proof-of-Work hash is the Merkle root of all your submission chain hashes. Tamper-proof. Employer-verifiable in one API call.
