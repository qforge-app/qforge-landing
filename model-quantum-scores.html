Methodology note: Scores reflect a combination of (a) QForge internal testing including observed code generation defects logged in session transcripts, (b) known model capabilities from public benchmarks and technical reports as of March 2026, and (c) published pricing. Qiskit 1.x accuracy scores were determined by generating VQE, QAOA, and Grover implementations and inspecting API usage. Llama 3.3 70B score of 4/10 for Qiskit 1.x reflects directly observed defects: 2num_qubits syntax error, use of deprecated result.get_statevector(), missing CX entanglement gates, and 3 unused imports in a single generation. Cost efficiency is normalized on a log scale where 10 = free (Groq free tier), 7 = ~$3/1M input, 4 = ~$15/1M input, 2 = ~$75/1M input. All scores are internal estimates and should be re-validated quarterly as models are updated.
